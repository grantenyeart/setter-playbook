================================================================
RAPID REACH - ALL POWER DIALER SCRIPTS
================================================================
One script per product. Each is self-contained: opener, pitch,
objections, book the Zoom, ending. (LLC/insurance = Lead Gen only.)
Also live at: https://grantenyeart.github.io/setter-playbook/


================================================================
RAPID REACH - LEAD GENERATION - POWER DIALER SCRIPT
================================================================
Goal: book the Zoom with a closer. Not to close, not to pitch everything.
This is the door-opener. On Lead Gen you CAN say the price the moment they
ask, it sells itself.

----------------------------------------------------------------
OPENER  (the original)
----------------------------------------------------------------
"Hey, is this [Company]? Perfect, quick question. Could you take on around
10 more details a week right now, or are you all booked up?"

----------------------------------------------------------------
READ THE FORK
----------------------------------------------------------------
IF YES (they've got room):
"Perfect, that's exactly why I'm calling. I work with detailers getting
15 to 20 extra bookings a week and I'm checking who can handle that volume.
I actually found you on Google and your reviews really stood out."

IF NO (they're slammed / all booked up):
"Good for you, sounds like you're busy. Quick question though: are you the
one doing most of the details AND answering all the calls?"
   -> If slammed, this is really your AI or DGS call. Pivot to those.

----------------------------------------------------------------
POSITION RAPID REACH
----------------------------------------------------------------
"What we built is a growth system specifically for detailing companies.
Some shops we work with do $10k months, some do $30k to $60k. We help
detailers scale no matter what stage they're at. Most detailers are amazing
at detailing, they just never build the systems behind the business that
let it actually grow."

----------------------------------------------------------------
THE PITCH  (when they ask the price, say it clean)
----------------------------------------------------------------
"We only ask 10% of your interior-exterior details. So for a $200 detail
that's $20 a lead, regardless of the job I send you, even if it turns into
a ceramic."

BACK IT UP IMMEDIATELY:
"We're bringing in 10 to 15 details a week strictly off getting your
company ranked number one on Google. And I cover all the ad spend, all I
ask is the 10% on the back end."

----------------------------------------------------------------
OBJECTIONS  (handle it, then steer to the Zoom)
----------------------------------------------------------------
"How do I know this works?"
-> "We're already doing this for detailers across the U.S. Higher rankings,
   consistent bookings, repeat customers."

"What if I don't make money?"
-> "Lead gen has no upfront risk. We only get paid when we send you
   qualified leads."

"I need to think about it."
-> "Totally. Is it the money, the timing, or just wanting to see how it
   works first?"

"Just send me info."
-> "Easiest way is a quick Zoom with one of our specialists, they'll walk
   it visually. Mornings or afternoons?"

----------------------------------------------------------------
LLC / INSURANCE CHECK  (Lead Gen ONLY, ask like the deal is happening)
----------------------------------------------------------------
Note: only Lead Gen needs an LLC + insurance (we're sending real jobs).
The other products do not require it.

"Before we get you onboarded, do you currently have an LLC and insurance
set up?"
IF NO:  "No problem, we'll send you the links to get that set up."
IF YES: "Perfect."

----------------------------------------------------------------
BOOK THE ZOOM
----------------------------------------------------------------
"I'll get a quick Zoom set up with one of our specialists who'll show you
exactly what it looks like and go over everything in a bit more depth. Do
you have any availability today?"

1. Push same day first. If no, go to tomorrow.
2. Pin a specific time. "Morning or evening?" then "What about 1pm / 8pm?"
3. Get their email, send the Zoom link now, give your number:
   "Text me if anything comes up."

----------------------------------------------------------------
ENDING
----------------------------------------------------------------
"Perfect, you're all set. One of our specialists will walk through
everything and show you exactly how we set this up for detailing companies.
At the end of the call they'll also send you a free full Google
optimization video. Great talking to you, speak soon!"

RAPID REACH - AI AUTOMATION / RECEPTIONIST - POWER DIALER SCRIPT
================================================================
Goal: book the Zoom with a closer. Sell the freedom, not the tech.
Pricing: give a range if pressed, then redirect to the Zoom.

----------------------------------------------------------------
PIVOT IN: when a booked or solo shop says no to more work
----------------------------------------------------------------
"We're booked" or "it's just me" is NOT a no, it's your cue to switch to
this pitch. A booked solo detailer can't answer the phone with his hands in
a car, so he's losing jobs he already gets calls for. Don't sell more
leads, sell capture.

SAY THIS:
"Honestly, that's exactly why I call the good ones. Real quick, when you're
out there mid-detail and the phone rings, are you catching those or do they
go to voicemail? ... Yeah, that's where the money leaks for solo guys. You
don't need more leads, you're already getting calls you can't physically
answer. We set up something that answers every one in your voice and books
it right into your calendar, so you stop handing those jobs to the next
guy."

IF THEY MENTION A SLOW SEASON (winter, etc.):
"And a lot of the guys we work with use the slow months to pull past
customers back in automatically, so it's not so slow."

Then go into the pitch below.

----------------------------------------------------------------
OPENER
----------------------------------------------------------------
"Hey, is this [Company]? Quick question, what time do you guys close?"

[let them answer]

"Okay, got it. And if I gave you guys a call past [that time], would that
go straight to voicemail, or do you have something in place that answers
that for you?"

----------------------------------------------------------------
READ THE ANSWER
----------------------------------------------------------------
IF NO / GOES TO VOICEMAIL (nothing in place):
   -> This is your call. Go into the pain and pitch AI.
   "Yeah, that's exactly what I figured, and that's actually why I'm
   calling. Every one of those calls that hits voicemail after you close,
   that's usually a booking that just walks to the next detailer."

IF YES (they have something):
   "Oh nice, what are you using for that?" -> If it's just basic voicemail
   or an answering service that doesn't book jobs, run the same pitch: the
   difference is ours books the job straight into the calendar.

----------------------------------------------------------------
THE PITCH  (after they confirm they handle calls themselves / miss them)
----------------------------------------------------------------
"If I told you I could automate that fully out, so every call gets booked
directly into your calendar and you never have to take a phone call again,
would that be something you'd be interested in?"

IF THEY HESITATE, GIVE TWO OPTIONS:
"We can either set it up full, so the AI answers every call and books them
in, OR we can set it up so it only catches your missed calls. You'd never
have a missed call again either way."

Lead with freedom ("never take a phone call again"), not tech. The full
version is the upsell, the missed-calls option is the easy yes. Let them
pick, then go straight to the Zoom.

----------------------------------------------------------------
OBJECTIONS  (handle it, then steer to the Zoom)
----------------------------------------------------------------
"How do I know this works?"
-> "We're already doing this for detailers across the U.S. Higher rankings,
   consistent bookings, repeat customers."

"How much is it?"
-> "Depends on your setup, so I don't want to throw a random number at you.
   That's the whole point of the Zoom, they'll show you exactly what makes
   sense for your shop and what it costs."

"I need to think about it."
-> "Totally. Is it the money, the timing, or just wanting to see how it
   works first?"

"Just send me info."
-> "Easiest way is a quick Zoom with one of our specialists, they'll walk
   it visually. Mornings or afternoons?"

----------------------------------------------------------------
BOOK THE ZOOM
----------------------------------------------------------------
"I'll get a quick Zoom set up with one of our specialists who'll show you
exactly what it looks like and go over everything in a bit more depth. Do
you have any availability today?"

1. Push same day first. If no, go to tomorrow.
2. Pin a specific time. "Morning or evening?" then "What about 1pm / 8pm?"
3. Get their email, send the Zoom link now, give your number:
   "Text me if anything comes up."

ENDING

"Perfect, you're all set. One of our specialists will walk through

bookings coming in, how are you keeping track of them all right now, and
how are you getting those customers to come back and rebook?"

      opening. Go into the pitch.
